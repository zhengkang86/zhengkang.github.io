About Me

I am a research engineer at Meta. Previously, I was a senior research scientist at PAII Inc. I received my PhD in Computer Science from University of South Carolina in 2019 and my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2012.

My research interests are mainly computer vision and deep learning.
