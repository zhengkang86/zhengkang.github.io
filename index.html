Brief Bio

Hi! I am Yirui Wang, a senior research engineer at PAII-labs. Before this, I got my Master's degree in Department of Computer Science at the Johns Hopkins University. I obtained my bachelor degree of Computer Science from East China Normal University in 2016, supervised by Prof. Aimin Zhou.


My main research interest is developing computer-aided diagnosis solutions for emergency trauma, especially on fracture detection in X-rays.
